Verolda

Verolda, goddess of life and nature.

Verolda is the goddess of life, fertility, and the seasons. She’s also the deity of widows, motherhood, and the seasons. She’s the wife (currently widow) to Andlat, and the mother of the deity Laekna, the goddess of healing and medicine.

Physical description and known manifestations

Verolda is usually depicted as an attractive young woman wearing simple farmer’s clothing and holding a sack of golden wheat. She’s said to manifest as farm animals.

Places of Worship

Verolda is one of the deities that retain temples, her temples usually contain blessed produce and are a place where farming and husbandry is taught, Her temples share the same golden heraldry as the minor temples of Viet’lan.
