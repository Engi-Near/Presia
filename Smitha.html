Smitha

Smitha, the goddess of the forge and craftsmanship

Smitha is a minor goddess, known to have been a dwarf of elven blood, and as such, she is shared between the pantheons. The elves perceive her as a goddess of the forge and trade with the dwarven kingdoms.

Physical Description and known manifestations

Smitha is manifested as a particularly short elven woman wearing a suit of heavy plate armor and holding a forging hammer, she is the least of the gods to contact mortals and as such little is known about her.

Places of Worship

Smitha usually has her temples close to entrances to the dwarven kingdoms where they would share prayers for the goddess of the forge.
