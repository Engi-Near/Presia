Andlat

Andlat, god of the death, lost souls, and sacrifice.

Andlat is the god of the dead, he’s the one responsible for the death and the afterlife of the elves. He’s the father of Ana and Charon, the goddess of souls and the god of Passage respectively. He is revered by all those that fear death.

Physical Description and known manifestations

Andlat is known to manifest as a cloaked figure where his features are obscured by fog, leaving piercing red eyes. He’s also known to manifest as a raven.

Places of Worship

Andlat is one of the gods that have temples. Though his worship is quite popular in cemeteries. His temples usually have his symbol (a black raven) in its heraldry and enjoy a grey scheme.
