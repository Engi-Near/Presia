Skjoldur

Skjoldur is the head god of the order Domain, father to the two deities Bjorg and Domeria (The deities of cities and justice respectively), he is revered by all those that respect civilization and order.

Physical description and known manifestations

Skjoldur is said to manifest as a large lion that can speak, he’s also known to manifest as an hourglass to represent his control over fate.

Places of Worship

As some of the other gods, Skjoldur doesn’t have dedicated temples. His worship manifests in courts of law, where justice is served. He’s also worshipped in offices or where the interests of the civilized world are pushed on forward.
