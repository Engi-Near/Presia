The Elven Pantheon

Elves are a very religious people, the very foundation of their society relies on the worship of the gods. The pantheon consists of 13 gods, dedicated to the various domains. The gods of the elves were once mortal beings, who walked the land alongside their own worshippers. After their passing into the afterlife, they ascend to godhood and watch over their people with keep eyes.
