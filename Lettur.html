Lettur

Lettur, god of light, the sky, and the cosmos

Lettur is the first of the ascended gods and is the strongest of the gods. It’s said that his death caused quite a change in the world, and caused the stars to disappear.

Physical Description and known manifestations

Being the oldest deity in the pantheon, it’s said that Lettur appears as an old man holding a cane with a star as it’s head piece. It’s also said that every 500 years, he manifests as a comet to look over the mortal world.

Places of Worship

With Lettur being one of the most important in the pantheon, he has temples in most of the major cities. His temples usually contain a lot of stars and celestial bodies depicted on its walls.
