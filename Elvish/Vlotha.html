Vlothan

Vlothan, deity of secrets, lies, and murder

A less favorite god between mortals, Vlothan is said to have been rather of a nuisance back when he was alive. Vlothan is a deity of pure chaos, who the mortals pray to in fear, for he might ward away his domains if they pray hard enough. Known as a major trickster between the gods, he’s usually a nuisance to them.

Physical description and known manifestations

Vlotham usually appears as a hooded person in a cloak that covers their body, only a few people have seen his face and it’s said that those who see it are be felled by 3 centuries of bad luck. He’s also known to manifest as a sole raven or a crow that appear in places where his domains are to be exercised.

Places of Worship

There are only a dozen or so temples for Vlothan, as people fear that too many would bring their attention to them.
