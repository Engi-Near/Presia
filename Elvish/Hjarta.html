Hjarta

Hjarta, the deity of compassion, lust, and jealousy.

Hjarta is one of the most fickle of the gods, as their domains include one of the kindest aspects, and one of the worst aspects of humanity. Hjarta has been known to amass a large amount of mortal lovers, most of which end up with gifts of prophecy or power.

Physical Description and known manifestations

Hjarta is a shapeshifter god, appearing as a young attractive member of either genders. He’s known to try and seduce those he feels an attraction towards. A distinctive marking of his is a broken heart (a realistic looking heart) tattoo on their back, which represents jealousy. It’s said that Hjarta can be pretty possessive of their lovers.

Places of Worship

Hjarta amasses large temples in most major cities in Alalsasaria, almost all of those temples contain a professional match maker that unites mortal lovers. It’s said that sometimes Hjarta themselves will hide as a matchmaker to unite those of immense love.
