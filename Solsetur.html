Solsetur

Solsetur, god of the night, discussions, and good company.

Solsetur, one of the oldest in the pantheon, is worshipped due to the affinity of the elves to a calm twilight where they can all talk and reminisce of their life. Solsetur is a favorite of the elves for that.

Physical description and known manifestations

Solsetur is known to appear as a satire holding a small lute and playing music into the night sky, those who follow his music are given a boon

Places of Worship

Solsetur is one of the few deities that are worshipped by the people of the fey and the elves, and as such, his temples are usually build near to fey crossings, as a gesture that the people of the fey are welcome in the lands of the elves.
